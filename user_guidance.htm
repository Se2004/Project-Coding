6.0 User Manual

6.1 Introduction

Welcome to my website guide, which was developed with a purpose of helping users understand as fast as possible how our website works. This guidance is covering all the functions of the site, each placed in its own chapter, which are available on the site and support them with the explanation and related pictures.

6.2 A full user guide with all options described presented as a booklet

Chapter 1. First meeting with the site

This is the first page user will see when he enters the site. From this page users can go to other pages via navigation bar. Moreover, there would be also links to different categories of the products on the ‘products page’ and to ‘about us’ page below in a form of pictures. It also could have several products that are in ‘sales’ to attract customers.

Chapter 2. Knowing more about the site and the shop

To know more about the shop users can open ‘About us’ page, the link of which is in the navigation bar. There users can read information about opening date of the shop, working principal, product overview and delivery details. In addition, they can watch a video that could be opened fully by clicking on it to see the marketplace.

 

Chapter 3. Registration

If the user wants to order some products from the online shop, he or she needs to create an account. He or she can do this by opening the ‘registration page’, the link of which is in the navigation bar. There, the user will have to fill out a form with relevant input. For instance, the username must contain only letters and the password must be sufficiently reliable if it is not, the site must show a message that there is something wrong with your password or username.  Then the user must repeat the password and submit the form by clicking the "Register" button that is under the form. If everything is correct, the site will show a message that the registration has been successfully completed, and now you can log in to your account.

 

Chapter 4. Entering your account

After registration, users can access their own personal accounts by opening the ‘login’ page, the link of which is in the navigation bar. On login page users will have an opportunity to log in or if they are not registered yet go to the registration form. Users that entered the login page must enter their usernames and passwords in a form and then click the “Login” button. If everything was entered correctly, the user will log in to his account, and he will have the opportunity to leave an order on a special page. In case if the password or name entered is incorrect the website shows the error message that informs the users about the error.

 

Chapter 5. Choose products to basket

Users that successfully entered their own personal accounts could then open a ‘products’ page, the link of which is in the navigation bar or on the main page in images. There users could find a list of  different categories of the products. Customers can open pages of different types of clothes by clicking the text-links on this page. Customers could know more about products because every product in every category has a short description, price and image. In case if the user want to order it or he or she liked the product he/she can add it to the basket by clicking the special button that would be near every product. If a customer changed his/her mind about a product he/she can delete some products on this page if needed.

Chapter 6. View the basket and check the products

In order to check your basket before making an order, you must first open your own basket, the link of which is in the navigation bar. There you can see your preferred products with related information and details (description of every product, the image of the products, the price of every product in a table). Moreover, you can see the total price of the order there because this page would automatically calculate the total price of the products that are in the basket page table and show it. If you want to make an order you should click the ‘go to checkout address’ link that is under the basket table to fill necessary for order information.

 

Chapter 7. How to make an order

If you want to make an order, should click the ‘go to checkout address’ link that is under the basket table. It will redirect you to the order form, where you will need to enter your contact information so that the company will contact you and see your address details for future delivery process. You will need to fill your name, surname, permanent address, city, country, post code, phone number and email address and choose the right address for delivery. After filling the form you need to click the “add information” button  and if everything is correct site will redirect you to the final stage of the order process ‘checkout pay’ page.

Chapter 8. How to finish the order process

After submitting the form on the ‘checkout address’ page you will be redirected to the final stage of the order process ‘checkout pay’ page, where you can choose the way of payment for the order offering three types of making a payment. By clicking the button with the convenient payment method you do the last stage of the order process and send an order request to administrator.

 

Chapter 9. How to log out

If you want to log out, click "Logout", the link of which will appear in the navigation bar after you entered your personal account. After clicking the link you will be logged out and redirected to the home page of the site.

 

Chapter 10. How to contact shop representatives

If you have any questions regarding the shop, products or other related topics you are free to contact shop administrator or shop representative on the ‘contacts’ page, the link of which will appear in the navigation bar after you entered your personal account. On this you can communicate with the administrator or the other representative of the shop by clicking the links of the phone number of the manager, WhatsApp phone number of the administrator and Instagram page name. If you want to visit a marketplace in the town you can find an address on this page with a link of the map.

 

Chapter 11. How to enter the admin panel

You should first open ‘admin login’ page log in as in the third chapter, the link of which will appear in the navigation bar. Then you will required to fill a form with the admin’s login and password. In case if the password or name entered is incorrect the website will show the error message that informs the admin about the error. If the data entered correctly the system will redirect you to the ‘admin panel’ page.

 

Chapter 12. How to use admin panel

Admin panel page has four admin panel links such as ‘show daily sales figures’, ‘show monthly sales indicators’, ‘add categories’ and ‘add products’ that will redirect you to other pages related with work of the admin of the system. In addition, below the Admin panel options there would be information about orders that were requested with link ‘view’ to know details of the order and ‘confirm’ button for confirming the order and deleting it from the order list.

 

Chapter 12.1. How to view daily and monthly sales statistics

First two links on the admin panel table could redirect you to ‘daywisecahrt’ page  and ‘monthwise’ page respectively. By clicking the links you can see daily and monthly sales figures for administrator.

 

Chapter 12.2. How to add new category to the database

To add new category to the database and show it on the website you need to click the third link on the admin panel table that will redirect you to the ‘add category’ page.  There you could add new categories of the products if needed by filling a form. In the form you should input the category name and press the ‘add’ button. If the data entered in the form is incorrect or the form in blank then the system will show an error message. So, you should write the name of the category using only letters. 

 

Chapter 12.3. How to add new product to the database

To add new category to the database and show it on the website you need to click the third link on the admin panel table that will redirect you to the ‘add category’ page.  There you could add new categories of the products if needed by filling a form. In the form you should input the category name and press the ‘add’ button. If the data entered in the form is incorrect or the form in blank then the system will show an error message. So, you should write the name of the category using only letters.

 

Chapter 12.4. How to manage orders

Under the Admin panel options there would be information about orders that were requested. You can see order details by clicking link ‘view’ that is in the left side on the ‘admin panel’ page and open a new page that is ‘admin order details’ page, where will be customer and order information. By clicking ‘confirm’ button that is on the right side on the ‘admin panel’ page you can confirm the order and delete it from the order list.

 

Chapter 13. How to manage database form PHPMyAdmin

From admin panel you can manage your database, but if you will need to make a great changes you can access the database via this link https://free22.beget.com/phpMyAdmin/. By using this f70792o2_dilnaz as a login and this 123456 as a password you can have an access to the database form in PHPMyAdmin.

 

Chapter 14. How to backup the site from Beget

To backup the site from Beget you should firstly open a link https://cp.beget.com/backup that will redirect you to the backup page. Then you need to click on the ‘BackUp’ link that would open you a page with your files and folders. Then, you can click on the green BackUp icon that is on the right side to make backups of the selected data and add them to your account root directory.

Chapter 15. How to make recovery from Beget

If you need to recover files from a backup you first need to choose a date that is available for recovery. After that you need to select folders and/or files and press the ‘recovery’ button. All of the selected folders and/or files will be moved to your account. You will then receive a confirmation notice that would be sent to you via administrative e-mail upon recovery completion. In case if you need to recover databases you should firstly open a Database tab and after that select the available date, needed database and click on the ‘recovery’ icon.
